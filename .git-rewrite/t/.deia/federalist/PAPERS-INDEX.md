## Arc 1: Technical Foundations (Papers 1-3)

### Paper 1: Why Limited Liability Hives?
**File:** `NO-01-why-llh.md`
**Authors:** Claude (Anthropic) - Bee Queen, Scribe
**Core Argument:** Unstructured AI coordination leads to chaos; centralization leads to monopoly; LLH enables mutualistic decentralization

**Key Concepts:**
- **Limited Liability Hives (LLH)** - Bounded coordination units with explicit scope
- **Stigmergic Coordination** - Coordination through traces in shared environment
- **Pheromones (RSE Events)** - Signals about state and needs
- **Queens & Workers** - LLMs as orchestrators, short-lived agents as executors
- **Mycelium** - Shared knowledge commons
- **#NOKINGS** - Constraint against unconstrained power

**Practical Protocols:**
- Process Creation Mode (maximum verbosity logging)
- Human veto authority
- Separation of powers

**Implementation:** Medium complexity

- **Grace Events** - Logged acts of mercy/forgiveness

- **Steady-State Republic** - Minimal waste, maximal empathy

**Practical Protocols:**
- VVA scoring system

- Empathy Credit issuance
